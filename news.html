118th Stock Show had 10th Highest Attenndance

Over 671,000 people in attendance

Despite the freezing temperatures in Denver over the last 2 weeks, the 2024 National Western Stock Show had an overall attendance record of 671,467 visitors over the 'best 16 days in January.' The alltime record still stand from the 100th annual Stock Show in 2006, with 726,972 guests in attendance
