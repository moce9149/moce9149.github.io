Who is Megan Moroney?

Megan Moroney is a Geotgia native, as well as a singer/songwriter that has completely taken over country music claiming herself to be an 'emo cowgirl'.

I look up to Megan as a role modle because of how much dedication and heart that she's putting into everything that she has done. She has showed that being a young girl in an industry that is mostly ran by men, can be challenging but it is worth it. Megans music pulls all the right heart strings and can have you crying to singing your heart out in the matter of minutes.
